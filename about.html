Hi! I am a designer and maker who is passionate about the user experience of emerging technology and the social impact of product design. Currently, I am a senior at Cornell University studying Information Science in the College of Arts and Sciences.

My interests range from politics and alternative education to computational and human-centered design. Through working at the crossroads of design and technology, I hope to contribute to innovations that will make experiences better for all people.

While I was born and raised in Michigan, I am a die-hard Boston sports fan (Go Sox) and faux east coaster. I'm a religious listener of Frank Ocean, frequent reader (and rare finisher) of Constitutional Framer biographies, and a committed DIY project enthusiast. At this moment I would drop everything to become a touring DJ (Major Lazer, call me).
