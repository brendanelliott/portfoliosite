Apple Summer Internship Project

UX Design Intern // Summer Project

Overview

Through the IS&T Development Program, I worked on the Customer Systems team as a UX Design Intern.

I lead the redesign of the conversational intelligence tools that leverage natural language processing and machine learning to power Apple’s Business Chat support service. My work is projected to increased the efficiency of dialog management localization by eliminating 91% of the previously required time, thus enabling Business Chat global expansion.
