Designing and Building a Record Shelf

Side Project // 8.18

Overview

One of my favorite hobbies is collecting and listening to records. While I can't claim to be one of those music wonks who loves the sound of vinyl over MP3s, I do love the experience of playing a physical record.

Up until now, I have kept my collection in a self-modded wooden crate from Joanne Frabrics. I wanted a storage piece that would more interactively display the records and also incorporated my record player. However, all the shelves online were either too expensive or did not fit my needs. So, I decided to design and create my own. This is my journey.
